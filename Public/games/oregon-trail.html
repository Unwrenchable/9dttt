📜 How to Play — 9D Oregon Trail

  • Goal: Survive all 9 Dimensions (30 days each) and reach Dimension Zero.
  • Travel: Click ▶ Travel in-game or press SPACE to advance days.
  • Events: Random events occur daily — disease, bandits, weather, rifts and more!
  • Resources: Monitor Food, Water, Health, Oxen, Ammo, Medicine & Morale in the HUD.
  • Hunting: Click 🦌 Hunt to open the hunting screen. Click animals to shoot them for food.
  • Store: Click 🏪 Store to buy supplies at dimension transitions.
  • River Crossings: Choose Ford, Caulk & Float, Ferry, or Wait at river events.
  • Dimension Crystals 💎: Collect crystals via rifts, crystal beasts & dimension clears for bonus score.
  • Danger: Running out of Food, Water, or Oxen (below 2) ends the journey!
  • Score: Survivors × 1000 + Crystals × 500 + remaining resources + speed bonus.
